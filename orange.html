Orange Flowers

Orange Agoseris

Orange is a rarer flower color and most orange flowers in colorado are not Colorado native. But this one is! The petals on this flower are a very fun shape. They are like spindly rectangles with a yellow outline. They stems are hairy and contains sap. They stems are thin and leaves are only really found around the base of the plant. They are found in meadows and clearings in mountainous areas.
